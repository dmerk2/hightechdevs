<h1 style="text-align: center;">Wordpress</h1>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Wordpress is a "Content Management System" that allows YOU as a business owner to make routine changes to your site. It also provides many tools for us to optimize your website and make sure you stand out against the competition.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">WordPress powers almost 27 percent of the entire internet. One recent iteration alone, WordPress 4.6 “Pepper”, has been downloaded 21.7 million times and counting. This is software you can <em>trust</em> that allows us to make powerful sites and then give you permission to change content -- if you want to! If not, we will gladly do it for you.</p>
